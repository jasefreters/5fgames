NIGHT SHIFT — ABANDONED PIZZERIA

You took the job because it paid more than minimum wage for doing nothing... or so they said. Three animatronics still walk the halls after dark. Keep them out of your office until 6 AM. Five nights. Three lives. Don't look away too long.

HOW TO SURVIVE

  • Cameras [C] — check the Stage, Parts & Service, and both halls. The animatronics only move while you're watching the cameras, so don't leave them up too long.
  • Look Left / Right [A] / [D] — peek down a hallway. If something has reached your door, you'll see it.
  • Flashlight [F] — while looking down a hallway with something at your door, shine the light. Their eyes burn and they retreat.
  • Miss it and you lose a life. Lose all three and you restart the week from Night 1.
  • The big one from Parts & Service is faster than the rest. Listen for the heavy footsteps.
  • Survive Night 5 to win. An optional Night 6 is unlocked after — beat it for a bonus point.
